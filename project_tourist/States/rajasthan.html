Hawa Mahal

History

Built in 1799 by Maharaja Sawai Pratap Singh. Made of red and pink sandstone.

Why Famous?

Known as Palace of Winds with 953 small windows. Designed for royal women to observe street festivals.

Travel Details

By Air: Jaipur International Airport (12 km)

By Train: Jaipur Railway Station (5 km)
